About the Mentorship Program

The P2PU Mentorship program is serious about bringing motivated people together to learn and mentor. We've made it super easy to mentor or be mentored, but we need help to keep it going.

Beautiful Progress Tracking

Mentors and students are expected to fill out a regular update about what they have done. We'll turn that into a fun timeline to share with your supporters and the mentorship community. Those who keep up with the program are eligible for additional mentorship opportunities!

Time Committment

A typical mentorship lasts for about 1-2 months (or more) and weekly conversations between the mentor and student. It's up to you to pursue the mentorship how you want, but we recommend you chat regularly and have a weekly phone/skype call.
